About LearnToCode.fyi

LearnToCode.fyi is an annual survey of developers who are actively learning to code. Whether you're learning to code for the first time, or you're a seasoned developer we'd love to hear from you.

We aim to be as transparent, open, and private as possible. The source code for this site is available on GitHub here. There are no tracking pixels on this site. We do verify an email address to prevent duplicate responses, but we don't share your email address with anyone. Your email will only be used to be notify you of results and future surveys if you choose to opt-in.

Results will be published publicly on this site once the survey is closed.

This survey is organized and maintained by Boot.dev.
