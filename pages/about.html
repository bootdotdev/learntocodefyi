About LearnToCode.fyi

LearnToCode.fyi is an annual survey of developers who are actively learning to code. While the majority of our participants are pre-first-job, we also welcome respondents that are just looking to learn new technologies, improve their skills, and maybe move to more senior positions.

We are as transparent, open, and private as we can be. This source code for this site is available on GitHub here. There are no tracking pixels on this site. We do verify an email address to prevent duplicate responses, but we don't share your email address with anyone. Your email will only be used to be notify you of results and future surveys (if you choose to opt-in)

Results will be published publicly on this site once the survey is closed.

This survey is organized and maintained by Boot.dev.
